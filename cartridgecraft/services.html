Services

We don't throw a motorcycle into trash when it runs out of fuel!!

Not only trashing would be incredibly expensive, but I'm sure you can imagine the environmental disaster that would occur. With your Printer Cartridges the scale is smaller; both in terms of costs and size, but the fact is...there's often no reason to throw your cartridge away when it's out of ink. The vital part of the cartridge is still healthy. It can still continue to help you print

Over 300 million ink-jet cartridges being thrown out each year creates one huge mountain of unnecessary trash! You can make a choice to have your cartridges expertly refilled, and you'll not only feel good about helping our environment, but you'll certainly feel good about the savings you'll pocket.

Now you don't have to thrash your expensive printer cartridges. We can reliably remanufacture and refill laser/ink-jet cartridges manufactured by all the major brands.
